About us

Welcome to Symmetry Skate, the online shop for custom street-snowboard skate products. Whether you shred a Freebord, longboard or a regular skateboard, our high quality decals will have you covered. I use these designs on my own board and I love everything about ithem: the freedom, the creativity. That's how Symmetry Skate was born.

At Symmetry Skate, you can find a variety of combined themes and colors for your boards. Our products are sourced globally, will be sent in two stages and may take up to 3 weeks to arrive. We'd love to see how these decals have transformed your boards and we upload these to our gallery for those interested! If you have an inquiry about an order please feel free to contact me with the button below and I'll get back to you as soon as possible. All stickers are made with high-quality vinyl that is durable, waterproof and easy to apply.

Whether you want to spice up your old board or give a new one some flair, Symmetry Skate has you covered. Browse through the shop and find your perfect decal!
