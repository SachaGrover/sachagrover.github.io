Prices

By reducing the fees taken for website maintenance we provide competitive prices, while paying tutors a fair hourly rate. With competitor tutoring websites charging approximately 30% in website maintenance fees for each tutoring session, experienced tutors find themselves charging increased hourly rates in order to offset the site fees. We wanted to create a fairer system, having capped maintenance fees at 7.5% for all tutors, regardless of your level.
